Biography

Evan (they/them) is a composer and sound artist working at the intersection of acoustic timbre and electronic processing. Their music investigates micro-gestures, rhythmic displacement, and expanded techniques for traditional instruments. Evan's works have been performed by chamber ensembles and featured in short films and gallery installations.

Recent projects include a string quartet written for the City Chamber Ensemble, an original score for the short film "Night Lights," and an electroacoustic suite blending field recordings with modular synthesis. Evan composes with an emphasis on clarity of instrumental color and live-electronics interaction.
